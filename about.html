About Us

We are a small travel business that provides holidays to different places at fair prices.Holidays can be booked for any specific time upto a month since we are a small business. We thrive to have customers from all over the continent and our office is based in the area that is just a few miles from the center of the capital.

How We Operate

You may have a look at the Holiday Packages page of this site and book your destination by filling in the form on the Book A Holiday page. We then send you an email asking for a confirmation of your booking, payment and then email you a ticket. The ticket we send you has all the necessary details for you to show when checking in at the airport and at the hotel.

Travel And Accomodation

Our prices are rather fair which cover the accomodation and travel costs altogether. So the price of a holiday package to wherever you are going includes the cost of your travel tickets(plain, train, car hire) and the accomodationwhere you will be staying. The ticket options available to you might be one-way and return.
